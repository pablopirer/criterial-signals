# Criterial Signals

Iberia-focused market intelligence for M&A boutiques, search funds, advisory firms, asset managers, and small investment teams.

Criterial Signals delivers concise, structured weekly and monthly briefs covering deal activity, competitive moves, real estate, and capital markets signals across Spain and Portugal.

## Status

MVP in early validation. Public website live at [pablopirer.github.io/criterial-signals](https://pablopirer.github.io/criterial-signals/).

## What it covers

- M&A deal activity in Iberia.
- Real estate movements and transactions.
- Capital markets signals.
- Competitive repositioning relevant to the target audience.

## How it works

Subscribers receive a weekly digest of relevant signals and a deeper monthly brief. A free sample is available on request via the website.
